Moving to Lektor

Almost nine years ago, I launched cecinestpasun.com as my personal blog. At the time it was a Django 1.0 site. I've used it intermittently, but never really paid any attention to it.

So, instead of persisting in the upkeep of a blog (and blog software) that is massive overkill for my small amount of publishing, I've moved the content over to Lektor, published as static content on Github Pages. I've used Lektor for the BeeWare homepage, and it's proven very enjoyable to work with, and it allowed me to port over my old Django content in a morning.

I'm hoping to to a visual refresh in the near future; but in the meantime, I can at least save some money on hosting.
